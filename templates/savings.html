What is G$ Savings?
  • 1
    Lock your G$ for a fixed period — from 1 day up to 1 year. Your tokens go directly into a smart contract on the Celo blockchain.
  • 2
    Funds are frozen for the duration you chose. No one — not even GoodMarket — can touch them. Only your own wallet can withdraw.
  • 3
    After the lock ends, click "Withdraw" on your save. Your exact G$ (plus any bonus) is returned straight to your wallet.
  • ★
    Short-term bonus: Lock for exactly 1 day (any amount ≥ 1,000 G$) and earn +10 G$ on withdrawal — paid from the reward pool if funded.
  • ★
    Long-term bonus: Save ≥ 10,000 G$ for ≥ 150 days and earn a tiered bonus — 1,000 G$ (10K–99K), 2,500 G$ (100K–499K), or 10,000 G$ (500K–10M) — paid from the reward pool if funded.

Smart Contract Security
  • Only the depositing wallet can withdraw — not the platform, not anyone else.
  • The contract has no owner and no admin controls. Reward pool funds can never be withdrawn by anyone — only used to pay saver bonuses.
  • All transactions are publicly visible on CeloScan.
