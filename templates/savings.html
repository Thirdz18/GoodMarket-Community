What is GoodMarket Savings?
  • 1
    Choose Weekly or Monthly for your lock period. Your tokens go directly into a smart contract on Celo Mainnet — no custom day input, just the plan that fits you.
  • 2
    Each (token · period) is a slot. If you top up the same token + period later, the new amount is added to the existing slot — the unlock date does not change. Weekly and Monthly saves open separate slots.
  • 3
    Funds are frozen until the unlock date — no one (not even GoodMarket) can touch them. Early withdrawal is not allowed; only the depositor can withdraw, and only after maturity.
  • 4
    After the lock ends, click "Withdraw". Your full slot amount (plus any G$ bonus) is returned to your wallet.
  • ★
    Monthly tier preview: Reward estimates update live as you type. Monthly tiers are based on your deposit size, and weekly rewards are derived from the monthly tier.
  • ★
    Monthly reward tiers: 1,000–4,999 G$ → 50 G$; 5,000–9,999 G$ → 250 G$; 10,000–49,999 G$ → 500 G$; 50,000–99,999 G$ → 2,500 G$; 100,000–1,000,000 G$ → 10,000 G$.
  • ★
    Weekly reward preview: Weekly rewards are derived from the monthly tier so the UI stays simple. The reward pool still funds payouts best-effort, and withdrawals remain available even when the pool is low.

Smart Contract Security
  • Only the depositing wallet can withdraw its slots — not the platform, not anyone else.
  • The contract has no owner, no admin, no pause, no emergency withdrawal. Reward pool funds can never be withdrawn by anyone — only used to pay saver bonuses.
  • Early withdrawal is not allowed — once locked, your tokens stay locked until the unlock date you committed to.
  • All transactions are publicly visible on CeloScan.
